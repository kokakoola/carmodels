    AURIS TOP FEATURES

    A more dynamic design

    Play movie Play movie View Hybrid

    ‘Ready for anything' - that's the face of the new look Toyota Auris. It's all aprt of a more dynamic design language. Whilst all Auris models share the same confident design, the Auris Hybrid features a range of unique styling features.

    Room for fresh thinking

    Play movie

    The new Toyota Auris is a driver’s car which doubles up as a practical luggage carrier with plenty of smart storage solutions. Featuring the added flexibility of a dual-level deckboard and 60:40 split-folding rear seats, all versions, including the Auris Hybrid, offer an increased luggage capacity of 360 litres.

    All eyes on innovation

    Play movie View Hybrid

    Whether it’s cutting-edge in-car technology such as the Toyota Touch & Go, Follow-me-home headlights or the latest Hybrid Synergy Drive® system – there’s plenty of room for new ideas.

    Expect premium quality

    Play movie View Hybrid

    High-quality finishes and refined materials create the wlecome atmosphere onboard the new Auris: nappa leather on the steering wheel, meticulous metal-like silver detailing, as well as stylish carbon trim and chrome inserts. The Auris Hybrid shares the same high quality interior as Auris with added hybrid-specific instruments, upholstery and detailing.

    Dynamic driving experience

    Play movie Play movie View Hybrid

    The new Auris benefits from enhancements to its bodyshell rigidity, suspansion, steering, driving position and weight management to provide an all-together more comfortable and engaging drive. The new Auris Hybrid’s HSD powertrain also features improvements to futher enhance the unique hybrid driving experience.
